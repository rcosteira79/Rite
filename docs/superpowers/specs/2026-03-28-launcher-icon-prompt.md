# HabitLock Launcher Icon — Design Prompt

## The App

HabitLock is an enforcement-based habit tracker that emphasizes accountability through user-defined strictness levels. It's not gamified — it's a premium, privacy-first tool that acts as a supportive scaffold for building discipline. Think of it like a father pushing their kid on a bike: it holds you accountable so you can eventually hold yourself accountable.

## The Concept

A stoic Atlas (the titan) holding up a todo list / checklist. Atlas doesn't put the sky down — he shows up every day. He carries the weight so you can build the habit. The todo list grounds the mythological figure in something mundane and relatable, making the icon both memorable and immediately communicative of what the app does.

## Tone

- Serious and stoic, not playful or cartoonish
- Premium and editorial — like high-end stationery or a wellness retreat
- Quiet confidence, not loud energy
- The figure should feel architectural and structural, not illustrative or whimsical

## Color Palette

- **Background**: Dark forest green `#163829` — the app's brand anchor
- **Foreground / figure**: Sage `#A9CFBA` — the app's primary accent
- **Optional darker accent**: `#2D4F3F` for depth/layering
- No synthetic vibrance — sophisticated, desaturated tones only
- Tonal layering for depth rather than outlines or drop shadows

## Style Constraints

- Must read clearly at Android launcher icon size (48dp / ~192px at xxxhdpi)
- Simplify and abstract the figure as needed for legibility — this is an icon, not an illustration
- Geometric and minimal over detailed and realistic
- No outlines or thin strokes that disappear at small sizes
- The checklist should be recognizable but not dominant — Atlas is the hero, the list is what he carries

## What to Avoid

- Padlocks, keys, shields, or anything that reads as a security app
- Organic/nature symbols (leaves, seedlings, trees)
- Gamified or wellness-app aesthetics (streaks of fire, trophies, hearts)
- Cartoon or mascot energy
- Bright or saturated colors

## Deliverables

- Android adaptive icon assets:
  - Vector foreground (`ic_launcher_foreground.xml`)
  - Vector or solid color background (`ic_launcher_background.xml`)
- Legacy raster icons for all density buckets (mdpi through xxxhdpi)
  - `ic_launcher.png` and `ic_launcher_round.png` per bucket
- Optionally: an SVG source file for future use (iOS, desktop, marketing)
